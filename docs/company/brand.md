# Brand Identity & Messaging

## Brand Positioning

Stamus Networks positions itself as the **transparent, open alternative** in the NDR market — contrasting directly with competitors who rely on opaque AI and black-box detection.

## Key Messages

### Primary Tagline Themes
- Defenders are heroes
- Clear visibility, clear detection, clear evidence, clear response
- Network security you can understand

### The "Clear" Framework

The product name "Clear NDR" reinforces the brand's core promise across four pillars:

| Pillar | Message |
|--------|---------|
| **Clear Visibility** | Monitor activities across your entire attack surface |
| **Clear Detection** | Multi-layer, transparent detections you can understand |
| **Clear Evidence** | Detailed attack timelines and complete evidentiary artifacts |
| **Clear Response** | Ultra high-fidelity threat declarations for automated response |

### Anti-Positioning

Stamus explicitly positions against:
- **Black-box AI** — "We question opaque anomaly detection systems"
- **Alert fatigue** — Declarations of Compromise (DoC) and Declarations of Policy Violations (DoPV) as high-fidelity alternatives to noisy alerts
- **Vendor lock-in** — Open APIs, Suricata compatibility, broad integration ecosystem
- **Per-device pricing** — Flat-rate licensing based on line speed, not host count

## Voice & Tone

- **Honest and direct** — No exaggeration or hype
- **Technical credibility** — Written by and for practitioners
- **Community-minded** — Acknowledges the ecosystem, not just the product
- **Understated confidence** — Let the technology and evidence speak
