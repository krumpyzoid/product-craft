# Core Values & Principles

## Philosophy

> "Cyber defense is bigger than any single person, platform, company, or technology."

Stamus Networks operates on the belief that security should be collaborative, transparent, and grounded in practical results — not marketing hype.

## The Three Rights

Stamus articulates its differentiation through three customer rights:

### The Right to Inspect
Customers deserve complete visibility into how their security tools make decisions. Detection logic should be transparent and explainable, not hidden behind a black box.

### The Right to Integrate
Security tools must integrate seamlessly with third-party systems. No vendor lock-in. Open APIs, standard formats, and ecosystem interoperability.

### The Right to Influence
Customers should be able to customize, extend, and contribute back. Detection rules, custom code, and community-driven development are first-class capabilities.

## Operating Principles

- **Transparency over mystification** — Avoid exaggerated claims. Apply appropriate technology to specific problems. Explain what the product does and how.
- **Open-source stewardship** — Actively contribute to and maintain Suricata and the broader open-source security ecosystem through OISF Consortium membership.
- **Defender-first** — Every product decision should make the security analyst's job easier, faster, or more accurate.
- **Evidence over assertion** — Detections should come with the evidence needed to investigate and respond, not just an alert and a confidence score.
