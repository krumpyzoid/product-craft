# Stamus Networks — Company Overview

## Mission

Stamus Networks believes in a world where defenders are heroes, and a future where those they protect remain safe. The company delivers network-based threat detection and response solutions that make security defenders' jobs easier and more impactful.

## Founding Story

Stamus Networks was co-founded by **Eric Leblond** (CTO) and **Peter Manev** (CSO), both deeply rooted in the Suricata open-source IDS community. Eric has been a Suricata developer since 2009 and serves on the OISF board. Peter leads Suricata quality assurance and serves on the OISF executive team. Together, they have contributed 10x more to the Suricata codebase and education than any other contributor.

The company was built on the conviction that network security should be transparent, explainable, and grounded in open-source principles — not dependent on opaque "black box" AI.
